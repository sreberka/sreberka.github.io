The story

Peter Maddox, 84, had been targeted in Bibury because he owned a yellow Vauxhall Corsa. A photo of his car was posted online, with the caption: "Picture postcard street photobombed by ugly little yellow car."

People visiting the village claimed it ruined their photos and someone even vandalised his car causing Â£6,000 worth of damage.

Hundreds of people on yellow cars came to support Peter. The car protest, which involved around 100 vehicles, happened in the same week Vauxhall said it would name their yellow colour after the pensioner.

Peter did not expect so many people to come to support him

HOW TO PLAY

In this game you are goin to support Peter and to catch some coins to repair his broken car!

Use the arrows on your keyboard to move your car

Be careful, otherwise you'll have to spend all the money to repair your own car! Good luck!!!
